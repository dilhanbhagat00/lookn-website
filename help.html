🌱 Getting started

What is Lookn?

Lookn is a free self-care companion app for iOS and Android. Your fluffy koko helps you track moods, build daily habits, journal, decorate a cozy dome home, send love to friends, and go on small wellness adventures. No ads, no doomscrolling, no judgment.

Is Lookn free?

Yes. The core app is fully free on iOS and Android. Lookn Plus is an optional subscription that adds more furniture, deeper insights, and exclusive adventures.

What platforms is Lookn on?

iOS App Store and Google Play. Your account syncs across both.

What ages is Lookn for?

Lookn is designed for ages 13 and up. It's safe for adults too — many of our users use it daily through stressful seasons of life.

🐨 Your koko

How do I name my koko?

You'll pick a name during onboarding. Tap shuffle for cute suggestions (Mochi, Sushi, Oreo, Cuddles, Pumpkin, etc.) or type your own. Rename any time from Settings → Pet name.

Why does my koko look tired or sad?

Your koko's mood reflects your activity. Complete a few tasks, log a quick mood, or do a one-minute reflection and they'll perk up. They're never disappointed in you — Lookn doesn't do guilt.

Can I change my koko's outfit?

Yes. Open the Shop and pick clothes, hats, and accessories. Equip them from your inventory. Some items unlock through adventures and quest rewards.

What are the different koko poses?

Your koko has idle, happy, thinking, dancing, walking, waving, petting, and flying animations. They cycle automatically based on what you're doing in the app and how things are going.

How do I pet my koko?

Just tap them on the Home or World screen. They'll do their petting animation. It also slightly boosts your bond.

✅ Tasks & quests

How do I add a task?

Home screen → tap the + button. Pick from suggested habits (mindful minute, gratitude, water, stretch, journal) or write your own title. Choose how often: daily, weekdays, or specific days of the week.

How do I check off a task?

Tap the task on the Home screen. Tap it once to complete. Your koko celebrates, coins fly in, and your streak ticks up if it's your first task of the day.

I checked off a task by accident — can I undo?

Yes. Right after completing a task there's a brief "Undo" toast. Tap it and the completion reverses. If you miss the toast, long-press the task to access more options.

What's the difference between tasks and quests?

Tasks are habits you set. Quests are bonus challenges Lookn gives you — daily quests refresh every day, special quests are bigger multi-step goals. Tasks are your routine; quests are the side missions that reward coins and rare items.

How do I claim a quest reward?

When a quest is complete, the card flips to "Claim!" Tap it. Coins and energy fly to your wallet, and special quests open a WishBox with rare items.

Why didn't my coins arrive after claiming?

Make sure the quest card shows the green check after claim. If coins still don't update, force-close and reopen the app — the wallet syncs from the cloud on next open. If it's still off, email support@looknapp.com.

🔥 Streaks

How do streaks work?

Your streak grows every day you complete at least one task. It resets at 4am local time if a full day passes with no completions. Streaks aren't punishment — they're a gentle nudge.

I missed a day — can I get my streak back?

Sometimes. Lookn Plus members get a streak repair to recover one missed day. Tap your streak on the Home screen to check if a repair is available.

Does my streak reset if I uninstall the app?

No. As long as you sign back in with the same email, your streak (and everything else) comes back from the cloud.

Why is my streak showing the wrong number?

Streaks read from your local timezone with a 4am rollover. If you traveled across timezones, your streak may look off for a day while Lookn realigns. Pulling to refresh on the Home screen kicks the sync.

🗺️ Adventures

What are adventures?

Adventures are short themed journeys — sleep, anxiety, gratitude, focus, self-kindness — that string together about a week of gentle tasks. They unlock rare decor and bond moments with your koko.

How do I start an adventure?

Open the World screen → Adventures. Pick a theme that fits where you're at this week. Lookn drops a few small tasks each day; complete them at your own pace.

Can I pause an adventure?

Yes. Adventures don't expire. Skip a day and pick up tomorrow. Your progress saves between sessions.

What do I get for finishing an adventure?

Rare furniture, bond XP with your koko, a memory card you can revisit, and a small celebration. Some adventures unlock secret decor that doesn't appear in the shop.

🏠 Your dome

What is the dome?

The dome is your koko's cozy home — yours to decorate. Walls, floors, furniture, plants, lighting, decor. Open it from the World screen by tapping your own dome.

How do I decorate my dome?

Tap My Dome → Edit. Drag items in from your inventory or the shop. Tap any equipped item to move, rotate, or remove it. Save when you're happy.

How do I buy furniture?

Open the Shop. Browse by category (furniture, floors, walls, decor, clothes). Tap an item to preview it in your dome before buying. Coins come from tasks, quests, and adventures.

Can I save multiple dome layouts?

Yes — Lookn lets you save favorite scenes and switch between them. Lookn Plus members get more saved scene slots.

Why is the shop empty / not showing items?

The shop needs an internet connection. Pull-to-refresh on the Shop tab. If items still don't load, check your connection and try again — your existing furniture is unaffected.

💛 Friends

How do I add a friend?

Open the Friends tab → Add Friend. Share your invite code, or scan/show a QR code. Once they accept, you'll see each other's domes on the World screen.

What's a friend's "feel" or boost?

A small bit of positive energy you send a friend by tapping their dome or progress card. They see it next to their koko. It costs nothing and bumps your shared bond level.

How do I share a task with a friend?

Open any of your tasks → Share → pick a friend. They get an invite. When you both finish the task the same day, you both get a bond bonus.

Can friends see my journal?

No. Friends only see what you choose to share — your koko, your dome, the tasks you explicitly mark as shared. Journals are private and never broadcast.

How do I unfriend someone?

Open their dome → Unfriend. The connection ends on both sides and any shared tasks close out cleanly.

Why did a friend disappear from my list?

Either they deleted their Lookn account or unfriended you. The Friends list refreshes from the server so deleted accounts drop off automatically.

✨ Lookn Plus

What is Lookn Plus?

An optional subscription that unlocks more furniture, more saved dome scenes, deeper insights into your moods and streaks, exclusive adventures, and comfort features like streak repair. The free app stays fully functional — Plus is for going deeper.

How much does Lookn Plus cost?

Monthly and yearly plans. Exact pricing in your currency is shown at checkout in-app, so it's accurate for where you are. There's a free trial.

How do I start a free trial?

Tap "Try Plus" anywhere in the app — paywall, settings, or feature gates. You confirm with Apple or Google, then you're set. We send a reminder before the trial ends so you can decide.

How do I cancel Lookn Plus?

Cancellation goes through your phone's store, not Lookn:
• iOS: Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions → Lookn → Cancel.
• Android: Play Store → profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Lookn → Cancel.

I cancelled but still have access?

That's normal. Plus stays active until the end of the current billing period (the date Apple/Google shows). You won't be charged again.

I bought Plus on iOS — does it work on Android?

Yes, as long as you sign in with the same email. Tap "Restore purchases" on the paywall if it doesn't unlock automatically.

How do I restore purchases?

Settings → Account & Backup → Restore purchases. Or tap "Restore" on the paywall screen.

🔒 Privacy & data

Does Lookn read my journal entries?

No. Your journal content is encrypted and never read by us. Not for analytics, not to train AI, not for marketing. The only time we'd see specific data is if you email support asking us to look at something.

What data does Lookn collect?

Account basics (email, anonymized auth), your app state (tasks, moods, streaks, dome), and anonymous usage events (counts like "opened journal"). Full breakdown on the privacy page.

Does Lookn sell my data?

No. We don't sell personal data, don't share with advertisers, and don't train AI on your content. If we're ever acquired, the same promise has to come with the deal.

How do I export my data?

Email support@looknapp.com from the address tied to your account. We'll send a JSON export within 30 days.

Where are my data stored?

AWS US regions. Encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Passwords are hashed — we can't see them, only let you reset them.

⚙️ Account

How do I sign in?

Email-based magic code. Enter your email → we send a 6-digit code → enter it. No passwords to remember.

I'm not getting the verification code.

Check spam/promotions. Make sure the email is correct. Tap Resend after 30s. Still nothing? Email support@looknapp.com from the same address.

How do I change my email?

Currently you sign in with a single email. To switch, email support and we'll merge the account safely.

How do I delete my account?

Settings → Account → Delete Account. Type "delete" to confirm. The app wipes your local data and deletes your records from our servers. Cloud cleanup completes within 30 days. Or email support@looknapp.com.

Can I use Lookn on multiple devices?

Yes. Sign in with the same email on iOS and Android. Your koko, tasks, streaks, dome, and friends all sync.

I'm getting too many notifications.

Settings → Notifications inside the app tunes which kinds (task reminders, streak nudges, friend activity) come through. Or use your phone's system Settings → Notifications → Lookn to silence the app.

Does Lookn work offline?

Mostly yes. Tasks, journal, dome, koko interactions all work offline. Friends and the shop need the internet. Your offline activity syncs next time you connect.

Still stuck?

Email a real human at support@looknapp.com. We answer in plain English, usually within a day.
