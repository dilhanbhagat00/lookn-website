A self-care app that doesn't feel like work

Lookn is a free wellness companion for iOS and Android. Track your mood in seconds, keep a private daily journal, build gentle habits, and watch your fluffy koko grow alongside your practice. No ads, no doomscrolling, no judgment — just tiny daily wins.

What you can do in Lookn

  • Mood tracking — log how you feel in two taps. See patterns over the week, month, and year without ever showing them to anyone else.
  • Private journaling — guided prompts or freeform entries. Your words stay encrypted, yours alone, and never used to train AI.
  • Daily streaks & habits — soft, non-punishing check-ins. Skip a day? Lookn doesn't shame you. The streak waits.
  • Wellness adventures — short themed journeys for sleep, stress, anxiety, gratitude, focus, and self-kindness.
  • Breathing exercises — guided patterns with calming visuals to anchor the anxious moments.
  • Friend mode — share daily wins with close friends, send high-fives, build habits together. Always opt-in, always under your control.
  • Your koko companion — a fluffy pocket pal that grows, naps, and celebrates as you take care of yourself.

Frequently asked questions

What is Lookn?

Lookn is a wellness companion app for iOS and Android — self-care, made fun. A fluffy pocket pal (koko) helps you track moods, keep a journal, and build tiny daily wins.

Is Lookn really free?

Yes. The core app is free on iOS and Android. A few optional features are available as in-app purchases. There are no ads and we don't sell your data.

Which platforms is Lookn on?

Lookn is available on the iOS App Store and Google Play for Android. The same account syncs across both.

Does Lookn sell or share my journal entries?

No. Lookn does not sell personal data, does not show ads, and does not train AI models on your journal content. Your words are yours. See our Privacy Policy.

How is Lookn different from other mood tracker apps?

Most mood trackers feel clinical. Lookn is built around a character — your koko — so checking in feels like spending time with a friend, not filing a report. Streaks are gentle, journaling is private, and the whole thing is designed to fit into a real life.

Can I delete my account and data?

Yes, in two taps. Settings → Account → Delete Account. Everything tied to your account is removed from our servers within 30 days. You can also email support@looknapp.com for an export or deletion request.
