      Privacy & your data

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      Lookn's privacy posture in three sentences: we collect the minimum we need, we never read your journal entries, and you can wipe everything in two taps. Here's the longer version.

      ❓ What we collect

      • Account basics — your email, a hashed credential (we never see your password).
      • Your app state — koko name, tasks, streaks, dome layout, adventure progress, friend list. Encrypted in transit and at rest, stored in AWS US regions.
      • Anonymous usage events — counts like "completed task," "opened journal." Tied to a random ID, not your name or email.
      • Anonymous web analytics — on looknapp.com only (not the app), via Microsoft Clarity, and only after you accept the cookie banner.

      ❓ What we never collect

      • The text of your journal entries. Ever. Not for analytics, not to train AI, not for support.
      • Your contacts, photos, calendar, location, microphone — unless a feature explicitly asks and you say yes.
      • Advertising IDs. There are no ads in Lookn.

      🔒 Who can see your data

      • You — through the app.
      • Our infrastructure providers (AWS for storage/auth, Apple/Google for app delivery). They process data on our behalf under their own privacy commitments. No one at these companies is reading your journals.
      • Us — only as aggregated anonymous numbers, or specific account data if you email support asking us to look at something.

      🎛️ Your controls

      • Export — email support@looknapp.com, we send a JSON copy within 30 days.
      • Delete — see Deleting your account.
      • Stop anonymous analytics — Settings → Privacy → Analytics → off (in the app), or Decline the cookie banner on the website.
      • Stop notifications — Settings → Notifications, or your phone's system settings.
      Tip: The legal version of all this is on the Privacy Policy page — the same words, more formally. This article is the plain-English version.

      💬 FAQ

      Do you sell my data?

      No. Not to advertisers, not to data brokers, not to AI model providers. If we're ever acquired, the same promise has to come with the deal.

      Where are my data stored?

      AWS US regions. Encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Passwords are hashed — we can't see them, only let you reset them.

      I'm in the EU / Canada / California — do I have rights?

      Yes. GDPR, PIPEDA, and CCPA all give you the right to access, correct, delete, and export your data. Email support@looknapp.com and we respond within 30 days.

      💌 Was this helpful? Reply to us at support@looknapp.com — we read every email.
