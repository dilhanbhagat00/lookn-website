      Deleting your account

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      Account deletion in Lookn is permanent and complete. Your data is removed from our servers, your Cognito user is deleted, your local app data is wiped. We don't keep a "soft delete" record.

      🛠️ How to delete your account

      1. Open Settings.
      2. Tap Account & Backup.
      3. Tap Delete Account.
      4. Read the warning, then type the word delete to confirm.
      5. Tap Delete my account. Confirm one more time.

      The app shows a progress screen as it wipes everything. When it's done, you're signed out and dropped on the welcome screen.

      ❓ What gets deleted

      Lookn paginates through every table on your account and removes the rows (500 items per page, 25 concurrent mutations per batch, up to 2 retries per failed item). The full list:

      • User profile (name, email, invite code, theme, locale)
      • Posts (journal entries) and Memories
      • Tasks and Task Stats
      • Pet state (your koko)
      • Scene state (dome, equipped items, owned inventory)
      • Streaks history and Progress Events
      • Adventures and Journeys (with Journey Days)
      • Quests progress
      • Wallet (coins), Shop Purchases, Daily Reward Claims
      • Preferences, Achievements, Notifications
      • Friend Links (both sides — your friends no longer see you)
      • Shared Goals and Shared Progress
      • Friend Boosts (both sent and received)
      • Your Cognito sign-in account itself (via Auth.deleteUser)
      • Push token (cleared during logout so the deleted account stops getting notifications)

      ⏱️ How long it takes

      The deletion runs synchronously while the progress screen is open. Typical accounts finish in seconds to about a minute, depending on how much data you have. When the screen completes you're signed out automatically.

      📦 About the "30 days"

      The live database wipe is immediate — your records are gone from DynamoDB the moment the progress screen finishes. Some encrypted infrastructure backups exist for disaster recovery for up to 30 days, after which they roll off too. There's no soft-delete flag and no archival copy on our side.

      Tip: If you want a copy of your data before deleting, email support@looknapp.com first and we'll send a JSON export within 30 days. Once you delete, there's nothing to export.

      🧹 After deletion

      You can sign up again with the same email — Apple/Google/our system give the new account a brand-new identity. The old account has no link to the new one; you start fresh.

      💬 FAQ

      Some data didn't delete.

      If you saw a "some data couldn't be removed" message after delete, your sign-in was still closed but a few rows lingered. Email support@looknapp.com with your account email and we'll finish the cleanup manually.

      I deleted by accident — can I recover?

      Within 30 days, sometimes — email support with the original account email immediately. After 30 days, the backups expire and recovery isn't possible.

      I can't open the Delete Account screen.

      Make sure you're signed in (Settings → Account at the top should show your email). If you see "not signed in," sign back in first, then try again.
