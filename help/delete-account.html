      Deleting your account

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      Deleting your account is permanent and final. Your data leaves our servers, your sign-in is closed out, and the app data on your phone is wiped. There's no soft-delete and no archive — once it's gone, it's gone.

      🛠️ How to delete your account

      1. Open Settings.
      2. Tap Account & Backup.
      3. Tap Delete Account.
      4. Read the warning, then type the word delete to confirm.
      5. Tap Delete my account. Confirm one more time.

      The app shows a progress screen as it wipes everything. When it's done, you're signed out and dropped on the welcome screen.

      ❓ What gets deleted

      Everything tied to your account. Specifically:

      • Your profile (name, email, your koko's name)
      • Every journal entry and memory you've saved
      • Every task, your completion history, and your stats
      • Your dome — equipped pieces and your full furniture collection
      • Your streak history (current, best, and every completed day)
      • Your adventures, quests, and progress milestones
      • Your coins, shop purchase history, and any unclaimed daily rewards
      • Your preferences, achievements, and notification settings
      • Your friend connections — your friends no longer see you in their list
      • Every shared task, every feel you sent or received
      • Your sign-in account itself, so logging in with that email is no longer possible
      • Your phone's notification token, so the deleted account stops getting any pings

      ⏱️ How long it takes

      The progress screen does the work right then and there. Most accounts finish in seconds to about a minute, depending on how much data you've built up. When the screen completes, you're signed out and back at the welcome screen.

      📦 About the "30 days"

      Your live data is gone the moment the progress screen finishes — there's no soft-delete or archive copy of your account. We do keep encrypted system-level backups for up to 30 days as part of standard infrastructure practice (disaster recovery), after which those roll off too. No one is reading those — they exist only to recover from failures, not to look at user data.

      Tip: If you want a copy of your data before deleting, email support@looknapp.com first and we'll send a JSON export within 30 days. Once you delete, there's nothing to export.

      🧹 After deletion

      You can sign up again with the same email — Apple/Google/our system give the new account a brand-new identity. The old account has no link to the new one; you start fresh.

      💬 FAQ

      Some data didn't delete.

      If you saw a "some data couldn't be removed" message after delete, your sign-in was still closed but a few rows lingered. Email support@looknapp.com with your account email and we'll finish the cleanup manually.

      I deleted by accident — can I recover?

      Within 30 days, sometimes — email support with the original account email immediately. After 30 days, the backups expire and recovery isn't possible.

      I can't open the Delete Account screen.

      Make sure you're signed in (Settings → Account at the top should show your email). If you see "not signed in," sign back in first, then try again.
