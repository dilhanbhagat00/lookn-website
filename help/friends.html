      Adding & managing friends

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      Friends in Lookn are people you've explicitly added via invite code or QR scan. You can visit each other's domes, send positive energy (feels), share tasks, and build a bond level together.

      💛 Adding a friend

      Every account has a unique invite code formatted like LOOKN-xxxxx, paired with a QR code.

      1. Open the Friends tab.
      2. Show your QR for them to scan, or tap Scan and point at theirs.
      3. Either side scanning fires a friend request. The other side sees a preview (pet name, username, avatar) and taps Accept or Decline.

      Duplicate requests are blocked — you can't accidentally double-add someone or send a request when one is already pending.

      ✉️ Accepting (or declining) a request

      Incoming requests show up in the notifications area with a preview. Tap Accept to link, or Decline to dismiss. Declining is silent — the sender isn't notified.

      ❓ What friends see about each other

      • Pet name, your username, and avatar.
      • Your equipped dome and pet (visible via FriendDome — read-only).
      • Your latest progress summaries (recent activity highlights).
      • Shared task progress on the weekly grid (yours + theirs).
      • Feels you've sent each other, plus your shared bond points/level.

      Never shared: journal contents (thoughts), private mood notes, your full task list, your streak history, or account info. The only mood data visible is what you choose to share in your own feed.

      💞 Bond level

      Each friendship has a bond level tracked in FriendLink:

      • Sending a Proud feel (Nice work, Dome love, Wrapped love) adds 1 bondXP.
      • Completing shared tasks also contributes.
      • You level up when bondXP ≥ bondLevel × 100 — visible on the friend's profile.

      💔 Unfriending

      Open the friend's profile → Unfriend. The link ends on both sides immediately. Cached friend data clears and your friend list refreshes from the cloud, so ghost entries don't stick around.

      Tip: If a friend disappears from your list, they probably deleted their Lookn account. Lookn cleans up deleted accounts automatically — you don't need to do anything.

      💬 FAQ

      Can I block someone?

      Unfriending is the main mechanism. If you need a hard block beyond that, email support@looknapp.com with the details.

      How many friends can I have?

      No hard cap. Most users keep their list to close friends and family.

      Can my friends see what I journal?

      No. Journal contents (thoughts) are private to you and never shared with friends or anywhere else. See Privacy & your data for the full list.

      Can I add friends by username?

      Not currently — adding is only via QR/invite code to prevent random adds. Share your code privately with people you know.
