      Adding & managing friends

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      Friends in Lookn are people you've explicitly added. You see each other's domes, you can send positive energy, and you can share tasks. Nothing is automatic — you're always in control of who sees what.

      💛 Adding a friend

      You need a friend's invite code or a QR scan.

      1. Open the Friends tab.
      2. Tap Add friend.
      3. Either type their invite code, or tap Scan QR and point your camera at their code.
      4. They get a friend request notification. Once they accept, you're friends.

      Your own invite code is on the Friends tab too — share it any way you like.

      ✉️ Accepting (or declining) a request

      When someone sends you a friend request, a notification appears in the bell icon. Tap it → Accept or Decline. Declined requests don't notify the sender.

      ❓ What friends see about each other

      • Your name and your koko's name.
      • Your current mood emoji (if you've set one).
      • Your equipped dome (so they can visit).
      • Any tasks you explicitly shared with them.

      Never shared: your journal entries, your private mood notes, your individual task list, your streak history, your account info.

      💔 Unfriending

      Open the friend's dome → Unfriend. The connection ends on both sides immediately. Any shared tasks close out. Both sides stop appearing in each other's friend lists.

      Tip: If a friend disappears from your list without warning, they likely deleted their Lookn account. The list refreshes from the server so deleted accounts drop off automatically.

      💬 FAQ

      Can I block someone?

      Unfriending is the main mechanism. If you need a hard block, email support@looknapp.com with the details.

      How many friends can I have?

      No hard cap, but the world map renders best with under ~40 friends.

      Can my friends see what I journal?

      No. Journal contents are private and never shared with friends, Lookn, or any third party.
