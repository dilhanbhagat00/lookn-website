      Repairing a missed streak

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      A streak repair restores a single missed day so your run keeps going. It's the safety net for the days that get away from you — and everyone gets repairs, free or Plus.

      🔥 Who can repair a streak

      Everyone. Free users get a small monthly allowance of streak repairs — usually 1 per month — that refills on the 1st. Lookn Plus members get a larger allowance, so frequent travelers and busy weeks are covered. You'll always see your remaining repairs on the streak screen.

      🚀 How to use a repair

      1. Open Home and tap your streak counter (🔥).
      2. If a repair is available, the missed day shows a Repair button.
      3. Tap Repair. Your streak fills that day back in and continues uninterrupted.

      🛟 Rules of repair

      • Each repair fills one missed day at a time. You can spend more than one repair in a row if you have them — there's no "only the first day" cap.
      • Repairs are limited per month and refill on the 1st. Plus members get a larger monthly allowance.
      • You can't repair the current day (it isn't missed yet) or future days.
      • Unused repairs don't roll over — they top up to the monthly cap.
      Tip: Repairs are the safety net — not the habit. Save them for travel days, sick days, and the unexpected. Most users only need one or two a year.

      💬 FAQ

      I missed 3 days in a row — can I repair them all?

      If you have 3 repairs available, yes — spend one per missed day to keep the streak intact. If you only have 1 or 2 repairs left, pick the day(s) to restore and the rest will reset the streak.

      I don't see a Repair button.

      Either you have 0 repairs left this month, or the day isn't actually missed yet. Tap the streak counter to see your remaining repair count.

      When do my repairs refill?

      On the 1st of each month at midnight, local time. Unused repairs don't bank — they top up to the monthly cap.

      Do Plus members get unlimited repairs?

      No — Plus gets a higher monthly allowance than free, but it's still capped to keep streaks meaningful. The exact counts are shown on the streak screen and in the Plus details.
