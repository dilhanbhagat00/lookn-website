      Repairing a missed streak

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      A streak repair restores a missed day so your run keeps going. Lookn gives you three ways to recover, in priority order: streak savers (earned from adventures), a one-time free repair, and a coin repair. The same rules apply to free and Plus users.

      🛟 Streak savers

      Savers are the first line of defense. They auto-apply when you have a gap — you don't pick them manually.

      • Earned: 1 saver every 2 adventures you start.
      • Cap: You can hold a maximum of 2 savers on hand at once.
      • Each saver repairs 1 missed day. If you missed 2 days and have 2 savers, both apply and your streak continues.

      🎁 The one-time free repair

      If your savers are empty, Lookn uses your free repair automatically — but only once, ever, per account.

      • It repairs 1 missed day. It does not stack with a saver to cover 2 days.
      • After it's used, you'll see freeRepairUsed permanently set on your account — it doesn't refresh monthly.

      💎 Coin repair

      Coin repair is the last option, and it's manual — you have to confirm it on the StreakRepair screen.

      • Cost: 1,000 glow gems (the in-app coin currency).
      • Available only when: you have 0 savers AND your free repair has already been used.
      • Repairs: 1 missed day. (For a 2-day gap with no savers + free already used, the streak resets — coin repair only covers 1 day at a time.)

      🚀 How to use a repair

      1. Miss a day. The next time you open Lookn, if you're inside the grace window, the StreakRepair screen appears automatically.
      2. If a saver or your free repair applies, Lookn shows it on the AutoRepairSaver or AutoRepairFree screen and the streak continues automatically. Tap Continue.
      3. If coin repair is your only option, you'll see Repair for 1,000 glow gems alongside a Start over button. Pick one.
      Tip: Savers come from playing adventures, so the more adventures you run, the more breathing room you build up. Most users finish a 30-day adventure with 1–2 savers banked.

      💬 FAQ

      I missed 3 days in a row — can I repair them all?

      No. Repairs cover up to 2 missed days (with 2 savers). Anything beyond a 72-hour gap is a full reset, regardless of how many repairs you have.

      I don't see a repair option.

      Either your streak is fine (no missed day in the last 72 hours), or all three repair paths are exhausted (no savers, free used, and not enough coins). In the second case, the streak resets silently — no screen appears.

      Do Plus members get extra repairs?

      No — the repair system is identical for free and Plus users. Plus unlocks other things (extra adventures, exclusive items, insights), but it doesn't change repair counts.

      How do I earn more savers?

      Start an adventure (Willow Woods is free). Every 2 adventures you start earns 1 saver, up to the cap of 2.
