      Adventures & journeys

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      Adventures are themed 30-day story cycles where your koko travels to a destination and collects discovery items. Each day you choose between two responses to a story moment and earn 150 coins. Available destinations: Willow Woods (free), Paris, St. John's (Plus-only), and more in development.

      ⚡ The energy bar (picnic bar)

      You need 25 energy to start a new adventure. Energy comes from completing daily and weekly quests — typically around 10 energy per claim. The bar caps at 100. Tasks alone don't grant energy.

      🗺️ Starting an adventure

      1. Open StartAdventure from Home (or the Adventures area of World).
      2. The Adventure Energy screen shows your picnic bar. Once it's at 25+, you can continue.
      3. Pick your destination on the Adventure Ready screen and tap Start Journey (or Start Flight for travel-ticket destinations).

      Only one adventure can be active at a time. You can't start a new one the same day you started the last (4 AM cutoff). The default destination is Willow Woods.

      ⏰ Duration & how it ends

      Each adventure day runs for 8 hours of wall-clock time. Lookn schedules a notification at the 8-hour mark.

      Completing tasks during the adventure cuts the remaining time: each task completion subtracts 30 minutes. So if you're heads-down working through your goals, the adventure wraps faster.

      When time expires (or you complete it manually), the Adventure Reward screen shows the day's discovery item and pays out 150 coins.

      📦 What you collect

      Over 30 days, your koko collects discovery items grouped by type:

      • Food & Drink, Nature & Wildlife, Landmarks & Places
      • History & Facts, Culture & Life, Shops & Markets
      • Games & Activities, Finds & Objects

      Items are saved per destination on your account and visible in your adventure log.

      🎁 Rewards summary

      • 150 coins per adventure day completed.
      • Every 2 adventure starts = 1 streak saver (capped at 2).
      • The discovery item collected each day for your log.
      • While at a destination, travel furniture for that location appears in the Shop.
      Tip: Starting an adventure is one of the fastest ways to bank streak savers. Two adventure starts = 1 saver, capped at 2. If you're going on a trip, start an adventure first.

      💬 FAQ

      Can I do more than one adventure at a time?

      No. Only one adventure is active at a time. Finish (or wait out) the current one before starting a new one.

      Why can't I start a second adventure today?

      You can only start one adventure per logical day (4 AM cutoff). The next start is available after 4 AM the next morning.

      Are there Plus-only destinations?

      Yes — St. John's is marked Plus-only and can only be booked with a Lookn Plus subscription. Paris, Willow Woods, and others are available to everyone.

      What if I don't have 25 energy yet?

      Keep claiming daily and weekly quests — each claim gives ~10 energy. You'll be ready in a couple of days at most.
