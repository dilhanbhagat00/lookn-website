      Your dome & decorating

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      Your dome is your koko's cozy home. You equip furniture into a fixed set of slots (bed, wall, floor, window, etc.), save scenes you love, and visit your friends' domes. Items come from the Shop, quest rewards, and adventures.

      🏠 Opening your dome

      Tap the World tab and select your own dome. You see a live preview at the top of MyDome, with your owned items grouped by slot below.

      🎨 Equipping furniture

      The dome is tap-to-equip, not drag-and-drop. In the owned items grid:

      • Tap an item → it equips to its slot immediately.
      • Tap an equipped item again → it unequips (slot goes back to default or empty).
      • Changes persist instantly — saved locally and synced to the cloud automatically.

      Every dome has the same fixed slots: bed, wall, floor, window, carpet, frame, light, door, front_left, front_right, back_left, back_right. You can't add custom slots — but every slot has dozens of styles and colors.

      🪴 The back_left slot

      The back_left slot is special — instead of one furniture type, it holds your pick from a small set of accent items: plant, coathanger, koala bag, e-guitar, golf clubs, or skis. Each has its own colors and rotations.

      💾 Saving favorite scenes

      Built a layout you love? Save it as a favorite scene:

      • Open MyDome → the Favorites tab in the owned grid.
      • Tap Save current to snapshot your equipped items.
      • Tap any saved scene card to restore it instantly — every slot swaps to that layout in one tap.

      Free users get a small number of saved scene slots. Lookn Plus members can save more.

      📍 Where furniture comes from

      • The Shop — Today's Picks refresh daily, plus the always-on Essentials section.
      • Quest rewards — daily/weekly quests can drop common or uncommon items; special quests drop rare/premium items via the WishBox.
      • Adventures — themed travel furniture (e.g. Paris items) only appears in the Shop while you're at that destination.
      • Defaults — every account starts with a basic equipped set (green wall, honey floor, mint bed, stone sidetable).

      👀 Visiting friends' domes

      Tap a friend in the World tab → FriendDome. Their dome is fetched live from the cloud and shown read-only. You see exactly what they have equipped — you can't change anything.

      Tip: The Shop's Today's Picks refreshes daily for free, but you can refresh manually if you want a new spread — 10 coins for the second refresh, +25 for each one after that.

      💬 FAQ

      Can I have multiple domes?

      One dome per account — but you can save multiple favorite scenes and tap to switch between them like outfits.

      My dome (or a friend's) isn't loading.

      Force-quit and reopen the app to re-hydrate from the cloud. A friend's dome can also look empty if they haven't equipped anything yet.

      Can I sell furniture I don't want?

      No — Lookn doesn't have a sell-back system. Unequipped items just stay in your inventory until you want them again.

      Why don't I see Paris furniture in the Shop?

      Travel furniture only appears when you're at that destination. Book a Paris adventure ticket (350 coins) — once you arrive, the Paris items show up in the Shop's Travel section.
