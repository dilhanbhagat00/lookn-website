      Your dome & decorating

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      Your dome is your koko's cozy home. Pick out a bed, wall, floor, lighting, and a few accent pieces — then save the looks you love and visit your friends' domes for inspiration. Items come from the Shop, quest rewards, and adventures.

      🏠 Opening your dome

      Tap the World tab and select your own dome. You'll see a live preview at the top, with everything you own grouped neatly by where it goes (bed, wall, floor, etc.) below.

      🎨 Decorating it

      Decorating is a single tap — no dragging required:

      • Tap an item and it pops into place in your dome.
      • Tap it again to take it off.
      • Changes save automatically, so you can switch things up as often as you like.

      Every dome has the same set of spots to fill in: bed, wall, floor, window, carpet, frame, light, door, two front pieces, and two back-corner pieces. You can't add new spots — but each one has tons of styles and colors to play with.

      🪴 The accent corner

      One of the back-corner spots is special. Instead of one type of furniture, you pick from a fun little crew of personality pieces: a plant, coat hanger, koala bag, electric guitar, golf clubs, or skis. Each one comes in a few colors. Great for showing off what you're into.

      💾 Saving favorite looks

      Made a look you love? Save it so you can switch back in one tap:

      • Open your dome → tap the Favorites tab.
      • Tap Save current to snapshot the whole layout.
      • Tap any saved look later to bring everything back in one go.

      Free users get a small number of saved looks. Lookn Plus members can save more.

      📍 Where furniture comes from

      • The Shop — Today's Picks refreshes daily, plus an always-available Essentials section.
      • Quest rewards — daily and weekly quests can drop new items; special quests drop the rarer stuff through the WishBox.
      • Adventures — themed pieces (like Paris furniture) appear in the Shop only while your koko is visiting that place.
      • Starter set — every new koko begins with a cozy basic kit so the dome never looks empty.

      👀 Visiting friends' domes

      Tap a friend in the World tab to drop in on their dome. You'll see exactly what they've decorated with — it's view-only, so you can admire without accidentally changing anything.

      Tip: Today's Picks refreshes for free once a day. If you're not feeling the spread, you can refresh it manually: the second refresh costs 10 coins, then 35, then 60, and so on.

      💬 FAQ

      Can I have multiple domes?

      One dome per account — but you can save as many favorite looks as your plan allows and switch between them like outfits.

      My dome (or a friend's) isn't loading.

      Force-quit and reopen the app — that refreshes everything from your account. A friend's dome can also look empty if they haven't decorated yet.

      Can I sell furniture I don't want?

      Not currently — there's no sell-back. Anything you don't want just stays in your collection, ready for the next time you want it.

      Why don't I see Paris furniture in the Shop?

      Paris pieces only show up while your koko is in Paris. Book a Paris travel ticket (350 coins) — once your koko arrives, the Paris collection appears in the Shop's Travel section.
