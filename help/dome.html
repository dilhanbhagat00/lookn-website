Your dome & decorating

Last updated May 19, 2026

Your dome is your koko's cozy home. You decorate it with furniture, floors, walls, plants, and decor that you collect or buy. It's a low-stakes creative outlet that grows alongside your practice.

🏠 Opening your dome

On the World screen, tap your own dome (with your name on it). You'll land inside. Tap Edit (top right) to enter decorate mode.

🎨 Decorate mode

In edit mode you can:

  • Drag furniture from your inventory into the dome.
  • Tap an equipped item to move, rotate, or remove it.
  • Switch wall, floor, and lighting from the side panels.
  • Save when you're happy. Or tap Cancel to discard.

🏠 Saving scene layouts

You can save multiple dome scenes and switch between them. Free users get a couple of slots; Lookn Plus members get more.

From inside your dome → Scenes → tap a slot → Save current. Tap an existing scene to load it instantly.

📍 Where furniture comes from

  • The Shop — daily refresh of items you can buy with coins.
  • Quest WishBoxes — drops from special quests.
  • Adventure rewards — themed decor unique to certain adventures.
  • Daily rewards — small bonuses for daily check-ins.
Tip: Equipped items show in your friends' world view too. A cozy dome is a tiny way to say hi.

💬 FAQ

Can I have multiple domes?

One dome per account — but you can save multiple scenes and switch between them like outfits.

My dome isn't loading.

Pull-to-refresh on the World screen. If a friend's dome won't load, it usually means they're temporarily offline or haven't equipped any furniture yet.

Can I sell furniture I don't want?

Currently no — but unwanted furniture just sits in your inventory and doesn't have to be equipped.
