Multi-device sync & offline

Last updated May 19, 2026

Lookn syncs across all your devices through your account. Use your iPhone, iPad, and Android phone in any combination — your koko, dome, tasks, streaks, friends, and journal all stay in sync.

☁️ Sign in on a new device

  1. Install Lookn on the new device.
  2. Open the app → Sign in with the same email you use on your existing device.
  3. Enter the 6-digit code we email you.
  4. Wait a few seconds for hydration — a progress ring fills as your data downloads.
  5. Done. Everything you've built is there.

❓ What syncs

  • Your koko (name, mood, outfits)
  • Your tasks, history, and streaks
  • Your dome layout, equipped items, owned inventory
  • Your journal entries and moods
  • Your wallet (coins), bond points
  • Your friend list and shared tasks
  • Adventure and quest progress
  • Lookn Plus entitlement (after restoring purchases on the second device)

❓ What doesn't sync

  • System-level notification settings (per-device)
  • Push tokens (per-device)
  • App appearance preferences set per-device (theme, etc.)

☁️ Offline mode

Lookn works without an internet connection for most things:

  • ✅ Check off tasks
  • ✅ Journal
  • ✅ Pet your koko
  • ✅ View and rearrange your dome
  • ✅ Update mood
  • ❌ Shop (needs internet)
  • ❌ Friend activity (needs internet)
  • ❌ Adventure progression (some steps)

Your offline activity is stored locally and syncs to the cloud the next time you connect.

🤝 Conflict resolution

If you completed a task offline on two devices at the same time, Lookn keeps the earliest completion and treats subsequent ones as no-ops. You won't get double rewards but you also won't lose progress.

Tip: If your data looks off on a device you haven't used in a while, pull-to-refresh on Home. That kicks a full hydrate from the cloud.

💬 FAQ

How many devices can I use?

No hard limit. Practical limit is wherever your account works smoothly — 2 or 3 devices is normal.

Lookn Plus only works on my iPhone, not iPad.

Tap Restore purchases on the paywall in the iPad app while signed into the same Apple ID. Plus is tied to your Apple/Google account, not the device.

I lost my phone — is my data safe?

Yes. Sign in with your email on the replacement device and everything hydrates from the cloud.
