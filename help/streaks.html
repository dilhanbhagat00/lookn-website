      Understanding streaks

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      Your streak is how Lookn tracks consistency — gently. It grows when you complete a task on a given day, uses a 4 AM rollover so late-night check-ins still count, and gives you multiple ways to recover a missed day before the streak resets.

      🚀 How a streak grows

      Complete any one task on a given day and your streak ticks up by 1 the next day. It doesn't matter which task, how long it took, or whether it was a shared task with a friend or a solo one — one check-in is the contract.

      You can see your current streak in two places: the StreakWidget on the Home screen and the dedicated Streaks screen in the Me tab (shows current streak, best streak, and a calendar with green dots for completed days and hammer icons for repaired days).

      ⏰ When a day "ends"

      Lookn uses a 4 AM local rollover. The day flips at 4 AM the next morning, not at midnight — so a journal entry at 2 AM still belongs to yesterday's streak. This matches how most people actually live.

      ❓ What happens if I miss a day?

      Lookn measures continuity in hours since your last completed task, not strict calendar days. Here's the grace window:

      • Under 24 hours since your last task — no missed day, no change.
      • 24–48 hours — 1 missed day. A streak repair fills it automatically if you have one.
      • 48–72 hours — 2 missed days. Two streak savers fill them both (auto). Otherwise, no path = reset.
      • Over 72 hours — the streak resets.

      That's roughly a 2.75-day grace window in the worst case. You're not punished for sleeping in or having a busy morning.

      See Repairing a missed streak for how repairs work (savers, the one-time free repair, and the coin repair).

      Tip: Travel across timezones can briefly look weird because the streak uses your phone's local time. It realigns once you're settled — no manual fix needed.

      ❓ What counts as a "task"?

      • Any task you added in My Goals (water, mindful minute, journal, walk, etc.) — completing it on Home counts.
      • A shared task with a friend, completed via the shared progress screen.
      • Tasks linked to actions (a Breathe exercise, an Affirmation, a Journal entry) — the action triggers completion.

      Things that don't count by themselves: opening the app, petting your koko, browsing your dome. The streak is about doing, not browsing.

      💬 FAQ

      Does my streak save across devices?

      Yes. As long as you sign in with the same email, your streak comes from the cloud and hydrates the same on iPhone, iPad, and Android.

      I uninstalled the app — did I lose my streak?

      No. Reinstall, sign in with the same email, and your streak (plus tasks, dome, coins, everything) hydrates back from the cloud on the HydrationScreen.

      The number looks wrong.

      Force-quit and reopen the app to re-hydrate from the cloud. If it still looks off, email support@looknapp.com with your account email and a screenshot of the Streaks screen.

      Does completing a task multiple times build the streak faster?

      No — the streak counts days, not actions. One completion a day is enough; more completions don't speed it up.
