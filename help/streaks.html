      Understanding streaks

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      Your streak is how Lookn tracks consistency — gently. It grows on every day you do at least one task, pauses if you miss a full day, and never punishes you for being human.

      🚀 How a streak grows

      Complete any one task in a given day and your streak ticks up by 1. It doesn't matter which task, how long it took, or whether it was a shared task with a friend or a solo one. One check-in is the contract.

      You can see your current streak on the Home screen as a 🔥 counter next to your koko. Tap it to see your full streak history — total days, longest run, current run, and a heatmap of the last few months.

      ⏰ When a day "ends"

      Lookn uses a 4am local rollover. The day flips at 4am the next morning, not at midnight — so a late-night journal entry at 2am still counts toward yesterday's streak. This matches how most people actually live.

      If you complete a task between 4am and your bedtime, it belongs to "today". After 4am the next morning, that task closes out and a new day begins.

      ❓ What happens if I miss a day?

      If a full day passes (4am to 4am) with zero completions, the streak resets to 0 on the next day. Lookn doesn't shame you — your koko just looks a little sleepy and waits.

      The good news: every user gets a small monthly allowance of streak repairs, free or Plus. If you have one available, you can restore the missed day from the streak screen — see Repairing a missed streak.

      Tip: Travel across timezones can briefly look weird on your streak. Lookn realigns to your phone's current timezone — a pull-to-refresh on the Home screen kicks the sync.

      ❓ What counts as a "task"?

      • Any task you added yourself (mindful minute, water, journal, etc.)
      • Any quest step that you completed by doing the underlying action
      • A shared task completed with a friend

      Things that don't count by themselves: opening the app, tapping your koko, looking at your dome. The streak is about doing, not browsing.

      💬 FAQ

      Does my streak save across devices?

      Yes. As long as you sign in with the same email, your streak comes from the cloud and is the same on iPhone, iPad, and Android.

      I uninstalled the app — did I lose my streak?

      No. Reinstall, sign in with the same email, and your streak hydrates back from the cloud.

      The number looks wrong.

      Pull-to-refresh on Home to re-hydrate from the cloud. If it still looks off, email support@looknapp.com with your account email and a screenshot.

      Does completing a task multiple times build the streak faster?

      No — the streak is one tick per day regardless of how many tasks you complete. Doing more is great, but the streak counts days, not actions.
