      Getting started with Lookn

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      Lookn is a self-care companion app for iOS and Android. You and your fluffy koko build small, gentle daily habits together — moods, journals, streaks, a cozy dome you decorate, and friends you can send love to.

      ⚡ Sign up in under a minute

      1. Download Lookn from the App Store or Google Play.
      2. Open the app, tap Get Started.
      3. Enter your email — we send a 6-digit code. No passwords to remember.
      4. Type your name, name your koko, and answer a couple of quick questions so we can suggest starter tasks.

      That's it. You're signed in, and your koko is ready.

      👋 Your first 5 minutes

      Try these in order — it sets the rhythm Lookn is designed around.

      1. Add one task from the suggestions ("mindful minute" is a great first one). Daily, anything you want.
      2. Complete it. Tap the task on Home, watch coins fly in and your koko celebrate.
      3. Log a mood on the Journal tab.
      4. Tap your koko on the World screen to pet them.
      5. Open the Shop and use your starter coins on a piece of furniture for your dome.

      You've now used five of Lookn's core loops in five minutes.

      Tip: Lookn is built for tiny consistency. 2 minutes a day beats 20 minutes once a week. Don't worry about doing every feature on day one.

      📍 Where things live

      • Home — your tasks for today, your streak, quick check-in.
      • World — your dome and your friends' domes.
      • Journal — moods and reflections.
      • Quests — daily and special challenges with coin rewards.
      • Shop — furniture, clothes, decor.
      • Settings — pet name, account, notifications, Plus, delete account.

      💬 Common first-day questions

      Do I need to pay?

      No. The core app is fully free. Lookn Plus is optional.

      Will my friends see my journal?

      Never. Journal entries are private, encrypted, and not visible to anyone but you.

      Can I rename my koko later?

      Yes. Settings → Pet name. Change it any time.
