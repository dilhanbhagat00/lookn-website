      Getting started with Lookn

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      Lookn is a self-care companion app for iOS and Android. You and your baby koala koko build small, gentle daily habits together — tasks, journals, streaks, a dome you decorate, and friends you can send positive energy to.

      ⚡ Sign up in under a minute

      1. Download Lookn from the App Store or Google Play.
      2. Tap Start my journey 🐨 on the Hello screen.
      3. Name your koko (the app auto-fills a cute suggestion — tap shuffle for more).
      4. Tell us your own name, allow notifications when prompted, and answer a few quick onboarding questions so we can tailor suggestions.
      5. Enter your email — Lookn sends a 6-digit verification code. There are no passwords; you log in by code each time.

      👋 Your first 5 minutes

      1. Add one task in My Goals ("mindful minute" or "water" are great first ones).
      2. Complete it on Home. You'll earn 5 coins and your streak starts ticking.
      3. Open Quests and finish a daily quest for more coins (and progress toward the WishBox).
      4. Visit your dome in the World tab to see your starter setup.
      5. Open the Shop and buy your first piece of furniture from Today's Picks or Essentials.

      That's the full loop: tasks → coins → shop → dome → repeat. Adventures and friends come next once you have a few days under your belt.

      Tip: Lookn is built for tiny consistency. 2 minutes a day beats 20 minutes once a week. You don't have to touch every feature on day one.

      📍 Where things live

      • Home — today's tasks, the streak widget, your koko in the dome scene.
      • Quests — daily, weekly, and special quests; the WishBox.
      • Journal (Lookn's) — moods, reflections, breathing.
      • World — your dome, friend domes, and adventures.
      • Me — your koko's profile, your streaks, settings, Plus, and account options.

      💬 Common first-day questions

      Do I need to pay?

      No. The core app is fully free. Lookn Plus is optional — it unlocks premium furniture, extra adventures, and more saved scenes.

      Will my friends see my journal?

      Never. Journal entries (thoughts) are private and not shared with anyone — not friends, not Lookn for analytics, not third parties.

      Can I rename my koko later?

      Yes. Open the Me tab and tap your koko's name to change it. No penalty — switch it whenever you like.

      What if I lose my phone or switch devices?

      Sign in with the same email on the new device. Your tasks, dome, streak, coins, friends — everything hydrates from the cloud.
