Sending feels & shared tasks

Last updated May 19, 2026

A feel is a small bit of positive energy you send a friend. It costs nothing, takes one tap, and bumps the bond you share. Lookn's low-effort high-warmth interaction.

Sending a feel

  1. Open the World screen or your Friends tab.
  2. Tap a friend's card, dome, or recent activity.
  3. Tap Send feel. Pick a vibe (warm, proud, high-five, etc.).
  4. Done. They see it next to their koko the next time they open the app.

What a feel does

  • Bumps your shared bond level.
  • Appears as a little floating heart/star on the receiver's koko.
  • Counts toward "send love" daily quests.

Receiving feels

When a friend sends you love, you get a notification (if enabled) and a small "feelz" badge on the Friends tab. Tap it to open the message, see who sent it, and reply with one of your own.

Sharing a task

Sharing a task is the next level up from a feel — you commit to doing the same thing together.

  1. Open any of your tasks.
  2. Tap Share.
  3. Pick a friend.
  4. They get an invite. If they accept, the task appears in both of your lists.

When you both complete it the same day, you both get a bond bonus and a small celebration. If only one of you completes it, you each still get the regular task credit.

Tip: Feels are perfect for friends you don't talk to often. One tap a week keeps the connection alive without the overhead of a conversation.

FAQ

Is there a daily limit on feels?

You can send one feel per friend per day, per type. It's enough to count, not so many that they feel like spam.

Can I send a feel to someone who isn't a friend yet?

No — feels need an accepted friend connection. Send them an invite first.

What if I miss the feel notification?

Open the Friends tab — the feelz badge sticks around until you read all unread feels. They never expire on you.
