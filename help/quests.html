Quests & rewards

Last updated May 19, 2026

Quests are bonus challenges Lookn gives you on top of your own tasks. Daily quests refresh every morning, special quests are bigger multi-step goals. Both reward coins and sometimes rare items.

🎯 Daily quests

Three small challenges every day. They refresh at 4am local time. Examples: "complete 3 tasks today," "send love to a friend," "spend 5 minutes in your dome." Tap a quest to see its requirement and current progress.

Each completed daily quest gives coins and sometimes energy.

🎯 Special quests

Longer multi-stage quests with tiers. Themes range from friendship milestones ("become friends with 3 people") to mastery ("complete 50 mindful minutes"). Each tier you finish unlocks the next, and the final tier drops something rare from the WishBox.

🎯 Claiming a quest

When a quest's progress bar fills, the card flips to Claim! Tap it. Coins fly to your wallet. For special quests, a WishBox opens and reveals what's inside — sometimes coins, sometimes a guaranteed item from a rarity tier.

Tip: Don't skip the claim. Quests sit at "Claim!" until you tap them — leaving them unclaimed doesn't auto-credit, and the daily refresh won't process the new quest until you tap the old one.

🎁 The WishBox

The WishBox is Lookn's reveal moment. Special quests and milestone events drop into it. Tapping it plays the open animation, shows you what's inside, and asks you to Claim — that's when coins are credited or items are added to your inventory.

💬 FAQ

I claimed but didn't get coins.

Force-close and reopen the app — the wallet syncs from the cloud on next open. If still off, email support@looknapp.com.

Can I redo a completed quest?

Daily quests reset every morning (so yes, every day). Special quests have multiple tiers — once you finish all tiers, that quest is done and a new special quest will eventually replace it.

What's "energy" for?

Energy fills your adventure bar. Once full, you can start (or progress) an adventure.
