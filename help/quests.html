      Quests & rewards

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      Quests are extra challenges Lookn gives you on top of your own tasks. There are three kinds — daily, weekly, and special — each with their own rewards (coins, items, or a WishBox surprise).

      🎯 Daily quests

      2–3 quests per day, drawn from a pool of 5 ultra-simple actions (complete a task, log a mood, breathe, send a feel, etc.). They reset at 4 AM local time and are de-duplicated so you don't see the same action twice the same day.

      Reward: 50/50 chance of 50 coins OR a daily surprise (a random common/uncommon item). Plus around 10 energy.

      🎯 Weekly quests

      1–2 longer quests per week, reset on Monday (ISO week boundary). Built-in anti-grind rules: once-per-day limits, unique action types, first-action-only counts.

      Reward: 50/50 chance of ~60 coins OR a guaranteed common/uncommon item, plus ~10 energy.

      🎯 Special quests

      Lifetime progress tracks — they never reset. Each has 5 tiers you unlock in order. Three flavors exist:

      • Pet friendship days — based on how long you've had your koko.
      • Furniture collection — how many items you own.
      • Action mastery — total mood check-ins, reflections, breathing sessions, etc.

      Reward: 100% WishBox with a rare or premium item, plus ~10 energy per tier claimed.

      🎁 The WishBox

      The WishBox is the daily reveal moment. It unlocks after you complete 3 daily quests, shown as a wobbling card on the Quests screen with a 0/3 → 3/3 progress bar.

      1. Hit 3/3 daily quests → the WishBox unlocks.
      2. Tap Unwrap → the WishBoxOpenScreen plays a short open animation.
      3. Tap Claim your rewards to credit coins/items and return to Quests.

      The WishBox resets daily at 4 AM along with the quests. Special-quest WishBoxes flow through the same screen but with rare/premium drops.

      ↶ Claiming a quest

      When a quest's progress reaches its target, the card flips to Claim. Tap it:

      • Daily/weekly: coins fly to your wallet, or the item rolls into your inventory.
      • Special: the WishBox screen opens. After claiming, middle tiers advance you to the next tier automatically; the final tier marks the whole quest complete.
      Tip: If you already own every item the pool would have offered, the reward falls back to coins: 20 for a daily surprise, 60 for a weekly guarantee, 300 for a special-quest WishBox. You're never empty-handed.

      💬 FAQ

      I claimed but didn't get coins or items.

      Force-quit and reopen the app — the wallet and inventory hydrate from the cloud on next open. If still off, email support@looknapp.com with your account email.

      Can I redo a completed quest?

      Daily quests reset every morning at 4 AM (yes, every day). Weekly quests reset Monday. Special quests are lifetime — once their final tier is claimed, that special quest is done forever.

      What's "energy" for?

      Energy is the picnic-bar mechanic for adventures. It caps at 100 and you need 25 to start a new adventure.

      Why don't I see weekly quests yet?

      Weekly quests start once you've used the app for a few days. They populate alongside the dailies on the Quests screen when they're available.
