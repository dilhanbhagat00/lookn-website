      Meet your koko

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      Your koko is the baby koala companion at the center of Lookn. You name them during onboarding, dress them in outfits, watch them animate through different moods, and grow your friendship over time.

      🐾 Naming your koko

      During the PetIntroName step of onboarding, the name field auto-fills with a cute random suggestion. Tap shuffle for a different one, or type your own (up to 20 characters). The default name is Koko.

      You can rename any time: open the Me tab → tap your koko's name on the PetCard → an edit modal opens. The subtitle reminds you "You can change this later" — there are no penalties.

      😊 Mood & animations

      Your koko has six Lottie animations that play in different contexts:

      • Idle — default resting state.
      • Happy — after a check-in or quest claim.
      • Thinking — during reflection prompts and loading.
      • Waving — greeting state, often on World.
      • Dancing — milestones, rewards, adventure completion.
      • Petting — when you tap them.

      You can cycle through the animations interactively on the SeePet screen — each tap moves to the next state.

      ✋ Petting your koko

      Tap your koko anywhere they appear (Home, dome scene, SeePet) to play the petting animation. Floating hearts appear briefly behind them — a tiny reward animation.

      👕 Outfits and customization

      Your koko has two clothing slots:

      • clothes_head — hats, headbands, accessories.
      • clothes_body — bow ties, scarves, full outfits.

      Buy outfits with coins in the Shop. Equipped items overlay on your koko in every view — Home, dome, friend visits. Premium outfits require Lookn Plus.

      💞 Friendship tier

      Lookn measures your bond by days since you named your koko, not by level. Tiers (shown on the PetCard with an emoji):

      • 0–3 days: Friends 🤍
      • 4–10 days: Pals 💗
      • 11–25 days: Buddies 💞
      • 26–50 days: Besties 💖
      • 51–100 days: Heartmates 💘
      • 100+ days: Forever 💝
      Tip: Your koko appears throughout the app — Home's dome scene, the Me tab's PetCard with a waving animation, and SeePet for the interactive tour. They're the constant.

      💬 FAQ

      Can I have more than one koko?

      One koko per account. Outfit changes give them a new look without starting over.

      Can I change my koko's color or species?

      Not currently — every koko is the same baby koala mascot. Outfits and dome decor are the personalization layer.

      Does the friendship tier do anything besides change the emoji?

      It's a milestone marker on your PetCard. There aren't separate gameplay perks tied to higher tiers — it's just a way to see how long you've been at it together.
