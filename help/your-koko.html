      Meet your koko

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      Your koko is the fluffy pocket pal at the center of Lookn. They grow alongside your practice — react to what you do, change moods, wear outfits, and live in a dome you decorate together.

      🐾 Naming your koko

      During onboarding you pick a name. Tap the shuffle button for cute suggestions — Mochi, Sushi, Oreo, Cuddles, Pumpkin, Mooshi, Boba, Sprinkles — or type your own. The shuffle never repeats the name on screen, so each press gives you something new.

      Rename any time: Settings → Pet name. Everywhere your koko's name appears (titles, quests, friend cards) updates instantly.

      😊 Mood & animations

      Your koko has several animations that cycle based on activity:

      • Idle — chilling. Their default look.
      • Happy — after a check-in or quest claim.
      • Thinking — during loading screens or reflection prompts.
      • Walking / waving — on the World screen.
      • Petting — when you tap them.
      • Dancing — milestones and adventure rewards.

      If your koko looks a little sleepy or sad, it just means you haven't checked in for a while. A single task completion perks them right back up.

      👕 Outfits and customization

      Visit the Shop and browse the Clothes tab. Buy with coins. Equip from your inventory under Settings → My koko. You can mix and match:

      • Hats & head accessories
      • Body clothes
      • Special seasonal items (during events)

      Some outfits unlock through special quests and adventures rather than the shop.

      Tip: Petting your koko (tap them on Home or World) gives a small bond bump and is free — do it daily for a happiness boost.

      💞 Bond level

      Your koko's bond grows with every task you complete, quest you claim, and adventure you finish. Higher bond unlocks new animations, special interactions, and seasonal goodies.

      💬 FAQ

      Can I have more than one koko?

      Currently every account has one koko. Outfit changes give them new looks without starting over.

      What if I don't like the koko character?

      Koko is the fluffy mascot — friendly, gentle, never judgy. If they're not your vibe, drop us a note at support@looknapp.com — we like hearing what works and what doesn't.
