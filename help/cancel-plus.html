Canceling or restoring Lookn Plus

Last updated May 19, 2026

Canceling Lookn Plus is a 30-second job, but the steps go through Apple or Google — not Lookn. We can't cancel for you, because we don't process the payment.

↩️ Cancel on iOS / iPad

  1. Open Settings (the iPhone Settings app).
  2. Tap your name at the top.
  3. Tap Subscriptions.
  4. Find Lookn in the active list.
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription. Confirm.

Plus stays active until the end of the current billing period. You won't be charged again.

↩️ Cancel on Android

  1. Open the Google Play Store.
  2. Tap your profile picture (top right).
  3. Tap Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.
  4. Find Lookn.
  5. Tap Cancel subscription. Pick a reason. Confirm.

↩️ Cancel during free trial

Same steps as above. Cancel anytime before the trial ends and you won't be charged a cent. Plus stays active for the rest of the trial period.

🔄 Restoring purchases

If you signed up for Plus on one device and don't see it on another:

  1. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID / Google account that purchased.
  2. Open Lookn → Settings → Account & Backup → Restore purchases.
  3. Or open the paywall and tap Restore at the top.
Tip: Use the same email address on both iOS and Android for the smoothest cross-platform experience. Plus from iOS works on Android (and vice versa) once you sign in.

💬 FAQ

I cancelled but Plus is still showing as active.

Normal. Plus continues until the end of the period you already paid for. Check Apple/Google subscriptions to confirm the cancellation went through — they show the "expires on" date.

I want a refund.

Refunds go through the store, not Lookn. Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com. Google: Play Store → order history → Request refund. We can't process refunds on our side.

I signed up on web — can I cancel there?

Lookn Plus is sold only through the App Store and Google Play right now. There's no web checkout to cancel.
