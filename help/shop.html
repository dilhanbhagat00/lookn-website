      The shop & earning coins

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      The Shop is where you spend coins (called glow gems in-app) on furniture and koko outfits. Today's Picks rotates daily, the Essentials section is always available, and Travel sections appear when you're at a destination.

      🛠️ How to earn coins

      • Tasks — every completion gives 5 coins by default (configurable per task).
      • Daily gift — claim 50 coins once per 24 hours from Home.
      • Daily quests — 50 coins each, or a random common/uncommon item.
      • Weekly quests — around 60+ coins, or a guaranteed common/uncommon item.
      • Special quests — rare or premium items via WishBox (no coins by default; 300-coin fallback when all items are owned).
      • Adventures — 150 coins per adventure day completed.

      🛍️ Today's Picks

      The shop's daily spread is 9 items, 3 per row:

      • Rows 1–2 (6 items, free): common, uncommon, and rare items — no subscription needed. Max 2 items per category to keep the spread varied.
      • Row 3 (3 items, locked): premium items, require Lookn Plus. If fewer than 3 premium items are available that day, the row fills with rare items.

      Refreshing manually: the first refresh of the day is free. After that: 10 coins, then 35, then 60, then 85… (10 + 25 × n).

      Tap any item to preview it on your dome. Confirm to buy — the cost deducts from your wallet and the item lands in your inventory immediately.

      📌 Essentials

      Below Today's Picks, the Essentials section shows 33 always-available items spanning every category (walls, floors, doors, beds, etc.). Essentials don't rotate — they're a stable baseline so you can always find something to fix up your dome.

      💎 Rarities

      • Common — the most affordable items; the most plentiful drops.
      • Uncommon — slightly pricier, less frequent.
      • Rare — mostly from quest rewards and the Today's Picks free rows.
      • Premium — locked behind Lookn Plus. Show up in Today's Picks row 3 and from special quest WishBoxes.

      Owned items show an OWNED badge; equipped items show a checkmark.

      ✈️ Travel destinations

      Travel is a separate Shop tab. Destinations include Willow Woods (free, default), Paris (350 coins), St. John's (350 coins, Plus-only), and others. Buying a destination ticket starts that themed adventure. While you're at a destination, exclusive themed furniture for that location appears in the Shop.

      Tip: Premium items in row 3 still appear during Today's Picks for everyone — you just can't buy them without Plus. Use it as a preview of what Plus unlocks.

      💬 FAQ

      I bought something by mistake — can I refund?

      Coin purchases are final, and Lookn doesn't have a sell-back system. The item stays in your inventory forever, so you can always swap it back in. For real-money Lookn Plus purchases, see Canceling or restoring.

      The shop is empty or won't load.

      The shop needs an internet connection. Force-quit and reopen the app, then check Today's Picks again. If still empty, email support@looknapp.com.

      Can I gift items to friends?

      Not currently — but you can send them a feel, which is free and warm.

      Are there any other currencies (gems, energy)?

      The only Shop currency is coins (glow gems). Energy is a separate thing used for adventures — earned from quest claims, capped at 100 — and can't be spent in the Shop.
