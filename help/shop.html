      The shop & earning coins

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      The Shop is where you spend coins on furniture, clothes, and decor. It refreshes daily with new items, plus rare drops from quests and adventures show up here too.

      🛠️ How to earn coins

      • Tasks — every completion drops coins.
      • Quests — daily and special quest claims give bigger coin bundles.
      • Adventures — adventure milestones drop coins and rare items.
      • Daily rewards — open the app each day for a small bonus.
      • Streak milestones — bigger drops at 7, 30, 100 days, etc.

      🛍️ Browsing the shop

      Open the Shop tab. Browse by category:

      • Furniture — beds, chairs, tables, lighting.
      • Floors & walls — change the base look of your dome.
      • Decor — plants, posters, small accents.
      • Clothes — outfits for your koko.
      • Special — limited-time event items.

      Tap any item to preview it inside your dome before buying. Confirm to spend coins and add it to your inventory.

      ✅ Daily refresh

      The shop's featured items rotate every 24 hours. Some items are permanent residents (basics always available); featured items change daily and disappear when the day ends.

      💎 Rarities

      • Common — cheapest, plentiful, often the daily refresh.
      • Uncommon — mid-tier coin cost, occasional shop drops.
      • Rare — quest WishBox and adventure rewards mainly.
      • Premium / Plus — Lookn Plus exclusives.
      Tip: Use the preview before buying. Some items look very different inside your specific dome layout.

      💬 FAQ

      I bought something by mistake — can I refund?

      Coin purchases are final, but the item sits in your inventory permanently — you can always swap it back in later. For real-money Lookn Plus purchases, see Canceling or restoring.

      The shop is empty.

      The shop needs an internet connection. Pull-to-refresh. If still empty, check your connection.

      Can I gift items to friends?

      Not currently — but you can send them a feel, which is free and warm.
