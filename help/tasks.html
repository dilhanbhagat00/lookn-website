      Tasks & daily goals

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      Tasks are the habits you set in My Goals. Each one can have its own repeat pattern, time-of-day reminder, coin reward, and an optional linked action (Breathe, Affirmation, Journal). Completing one task a day grows your streak.

      ✅ Adding a task

      1. Open My Goals → tap Add task.
      2. Pick a category: easy wins, movement, hygiene, nutrition, mindfulness, connection, creativity, or learning.
      3. Set the title and an emoji icon.
      4. Optionally: pick a time of day for a reminder, set times per day, link an exercise (Breathe / Affirmation / Journal), and tweak the coin reward (default 5 coins).
      5. Pick a repeat cycle (see below) and save.

      🔁 Repeat cycles

      • Daily — every N days (default every 1 day).
      • Weekly — every N weeks, with optional specific days (e.g. Mon/Wed/Fri).
      • Monthly — every N months.
      • Does not repeat — a one-time task on a specific due date.

      Lookn doesn't show tasks on days they're not scheduled. A weekday-only task won't bug you on Saturday — and your streak still grows on weekend days as long as some other task is completed.

      ✅ Completing a task

      Tap the task on Home. Your wallet gets the coin reward immediately, a haptic confirms, and the task disappears from today's list (it'll come back tomorrow if it repeats).

      Tasks with a linked exercise work differently: tapping them opens the exercise (Breathe, Affirmation, Journal), and completion happens automatically when the exercise finishes.

      ↶ Undo a completion

      You have a 30-second window right after completion to undo. Tap the undo button → the completion is cleared, the task reappears, and coins are reversed. Only the last completed task can be undone, and only within the 30s window.

      ✏️ Editing or deleting a task

      Open My Goals → tap a task to edit any field (title, category, time, repeat cycle, points, icon, linked exercise). Most edits auto-save immediately.

      To delete: open the task and tap Delete → confirm. It's gone everywhere — from your list, from any scheduled reminders, and from your account. Your past completion history stays.

      🪄 Smart sort

      Switch the sort mode to Smart and Lookn uses a bit of AI to prioritize your day. It groups today's tasks into three buckets:

      • Do now — the things most likely to fit right now, based on time of day and how you've used the app before.
      • If you have energy — solid second-tier picks.
      • Anytime — the flexible ones that can wait.

      If something stops the AI from running, Smart sort falls back to yesterday's groupings, then to a plain time-of-day sort. You can also pick Time of day or Custom ordering from the sort menu instead.

      🤝 Sharing a task with a friend

      From a task, tap Share → pick a friend. They get an invite; once they accept, the task becomes active for both of you. A shared weekly grid shows both your completions across the past 7 days. Your individual completion still counts toward your own streak.

      Tip: Start with 3–5 tasks max. More than that and you'll dread opening the app. The point is to make the rhythm stick — you can always add more.

      💬 FAQ

      How many tasks can I have?

      No hard limit, but anything over 8 active daily tasks tends to feel like work instead of self-care.

      What's the difference between tasks and quests?

      Tasks are habits you set. Quests are challenges Lookn gives you. Tasks build your routine; quests reward you for variety and milestones.

      Why didn't my streak go up after completing a task?

      The streak counts days, not actions. One completion today is enough. If you already completed another task today, this one didn't bump it again — but it still gave you coins.

      Can I change the coin reward per task?

      Yes — open the task in My Goals and set points to whatever you want (default 5). Bigger rewards on harder tasks make sense.
