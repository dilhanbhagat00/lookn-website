Tasks & daily goals

Last updated May 19, 2026

Tasks are the habits you set for yourself. They're the heart of Lookn — the thing your streak counts and the thing your koko reacts to.

Adding a task

  1. Open the Home screen.
  2. Tap the + button (bottom right).
  3. Choose from suggestions (mindful minute, gratitude, water, stretch, journal) or tap Custom to write your own.
  4. Pick how often: daily, weekdays, weekends, or specific days of the week.
  5. Tap Save.

Completing a task

Tap the task once on Home. Your koko celebrates, coins fly to your wallet, and your streak ticks up (if it's your first task of the day).

Tap a completed task again to see the success state — completion time, coin reward, and the option to undo if you tapped by mistake.

Undo a completion

After completing a task, a brief Undo toast appears at the bottom. Tap it within ~5 seconds to reverse the completion. Coins return, the streak adjusts, and the task goes back to pending.

If you miss the toast, long-press the task to access the same options.

Editing or deleting a task

Long-press any task → Edit or Delete. Edits take effect immediately; deletion is permanent (but past completions and the streak record remain).

Repeat cycles & days off

Lookn doesn't bug you for tasks on days they're not scheduled. Set a task to weekdays only and it'll quietly skip Saturday and Sunday — your streak still grows on weekend days as long as some other task is done.

Smart sort

By default tasks sort by time of day (morning routines first, evening last). You can switch to Smart sort (Lookn surfaces what you're most likely to skip today) or Custom (drag to reorder) under Settings → Task sorting.

Tip: Start with 3–5 tasks max. More than that and you'll dread opening the app. Add more once the rhythm sticks.

FAQ

How many tasks can I have?

No hard limit, but anything over 8 active daily tasks tends to feel like work instead of self-care. Quality over quantity.

What's the difference between tasks and quests?

Tasks are habits you set. Quests are challenges Lookn gives you. Tasks build your routine; quests reward you for variety and milestones.

Why didn't my streak go up after completing a task?

The streak only ticks once per day. If you already completed another task today, this one didn't bump it again — but it still counts toward bond and coins.
