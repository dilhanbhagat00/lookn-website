      What is Lookn Plus?

      Last updated May 19, 2026

      Lookn Plus is the optional subscription. It unlocks premium furniture, exclusive adventure destinations, more saved scenes, and Insights. The free app stays fully usable — Plus is for going deeper. Note: streak repair is the same for everyone, free or Plus.

      ❓ What you get with Plus

      • Premium furniture — the locked Row 3 of the Shop's Today's Picks, plus exclusive items via special-quest WishBoxes.
      • Premium feels — seasonal and non-seasonal premium feels (Tulip wishes, Matcha mood, Rose love, etc.).
      • Exclusive adventures — destinations like St. John's are Plus-only. Paris and Willow Woods stay free.
      • More saved dome scenes — free users get a few favorite scene slots; Plus members get more.
      • Insights — mood patterns, task completion trends, emotional patterns.
      • Premium koko outfits — exclusive head and body items in the Shop's clothes section.

      What Plus does NOT change: streaks, streak savers, the free repair, or coin repair mechanics. Those are identical for free and Plus users.

      💳 Plans & pricing

      • Monthly — $9.99 / month.
      • Annual — $59.99 / year (marked "BEST DEAL" in the plan selector).

      Exact local pricing is shown at checkout — the App Store and Google Play handle the transaction in your currency.

      🎁 Free trial

      Plus includes a 3-day free trial. Tap Start free trial on the paywall, confirm with Apple/Google, and full access starts immediately. Billing begins on day 4 unless you cancel before then.

      ↩️ Canceling

      See Canceling or restoring Lookn Plus — the steps live on Apple or Google's side, not in Lookn.

      Tip: You can cancel the day after you start. Apple/Google won't bill until the trial ends. Cancel anytime before the renewal date and you keep all 3 free days.

      💬 FAQ

      Will I lose data if I cancel Plus?

      No. Cancellation just stops the renewal. Your koko, dome, streak, friends, tasks all stay. Plus-only items remain in your inventory but you won't be able to buy new ones until you resubscribe.

      Can I get a refund?

      Refunds go through Apple or Google, not Lookn. Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com. Google: Play Store → order history → Request refund.

      Does Plus auto-renew?

      Yes — that's the App Store / Play Store default. You can turn off auto-renew in your store subscriptions settings; Plus stays active until the end of the period you've already paid for.

      Does Plus give me extra streak savers?

      No. Streak savers come only from adventures (1 per 2 adventure starts, max 2). The repair system is identical for free and Plus.
